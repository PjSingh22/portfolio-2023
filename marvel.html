Rank #5 - Thor: Ragnorak

Previous
Next

*Spoiler Warning*

Thor Ragnarok provides a brand new character arc for the God of Thunder, bringing light hearted humor to the table, while breaking down everything we used to know about the character. In this movie, Thor becomes less Shakespearean, loses his hammer, and has to find himself as a person, which overall, makes him a more relatable character. Throughout the course of this film, Thor finds himself losing more and more about what made him, him. With that, he must reevaluate who he is. What I enjoy about this arc is that he doesnâ€™t find the answer to that by the end of this movie, but instead, this idea continues to get explored in his future movies, which shows the real impact of the obstacles he has faced. With the first two Thor movies being not so spectacular, rather than being the end of a trilogy, Thor Ragnarok brings a refreshing start to the character, which continues on in his following movies, and really shows us how much more Thor can grow as a person.
